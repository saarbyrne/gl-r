Glór

Empowering Irish Voices, Together — For a Fairer, More Inclusive Democracy

Problem

Public consultations in Ireland often fail to reach or engage a wide range of voices. Traditional methods — such as lengthy reports, static surveys, or town hall meetings — are complex, time-consuming, and tend to favour the same few, well-organised participants. Marginalised groups, younger citizens, and rural communities are frequently left out of these conversations.

Existing digital platforms like pol.is show promise but fall short on accessibility, engagement, and user experience. This results in limited impact, poor data quality, and a missed opportunity to strengthen democratic engagement at a time when public trust in institutions is fragile.

Our Solution

Glór is an interactive, user-friendly digital platform designed to reimagine how Irish citizens engage in public consultations. We simplify participation, offering conversational, accessible tools for people to share their views while helping policymakers collect richer, more meaningful insights.

Our platform is designed for Ireland’s unique context — capturing regional issues, cultural nuances, and supporting both English and Irish language use. We enable real-time participation while visualising emerging consensus, disagreements, and new ideas — all in an open, transparent environment.

How It Works

  • Conversational Flow: Intuitive, question-driven engagement replacing long surveys or static forms.
  • Real-time Clusters: Visual maps show areas of agreement, disagreement, and neutral zones as people respond.
  • AI-Powered Summaries: Automatic analysis surfaces key themes, emerging consensus, and contested ideas for policymakers.
  • Irish Context: Built around Irish issues, accessibility needs, and optional Irish language participation.

Who Benefits

  • Citizens: Feel heard and valued — no complex forms or inaccessible processes.
  • Government & Councils: Access rich, structured insights they can act on — not just raw data.
  • Communities & NGOs: Use the platform to raise awareness of local issues and organise around shared concerns.
  • Youth, Marginalised Groups & Rural Voices: Finally see themselves reflected in conversations about policy and public services.

Our Vision

We’re building Glór to create a healthier, more inclusive democracy — where public participation is simple, engaging, and representative. By making consultation more interactive, we aim to rebuild trust between people and decision-makers, ensuring diverse voices shape Ireland’s future policies and services.

Our goal is to become the standard civic engagement tool for local councils, national agencies, and community organisations — designed in Ireland, for Ireland, but scalable globally.
